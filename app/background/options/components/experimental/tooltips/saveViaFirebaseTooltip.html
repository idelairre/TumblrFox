* Enabling will cause clicking "Save cache" to upload your cached data to Firebase and clicking "Restore cache" to download your posts from Firebase. Otherwise clicking "Save cache" will assemble a fileblob and download it while "Restore cache" will allow you to select a file containing cached posts which TumblrFox will attempt to parse and inject into the database.
  • Advantages: Much more stable than assembling in the browser
  • Drawbacks: Slow/sometimes unavailable due to data limits
